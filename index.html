SRI LANKA

Sri Lanka, before called as Ceylon is a country which is an island, shaped like a teardrop located in the coast of India in Indian Ocean and called as the ‘Pearl of the Indian ocean’. Despite the fact of being an island, the country has its own way of natural beauty filled with beaches, an amazing wildlife with an extraordinary biodiversity and the ancient ruins which tell us the stories about the nation’s past.
